FAQ

Common questions

How does the fixed-fee model work?
After a free 30-minute consultation, we send a written scope with a fixed fee and timeline. You approve before any work begins, and the fee doesn't change unless the scope does — never hourly billing.
We already have a CA. Why do we need Ofstride?
Most MSMEs juggle a CA, a lawyer, an HR consultant and an IT vendor separately. We coordinate all of it under one accountable team — and use AI to deliver faster at lower cost. We're happy to work alongside your existing CA too.
What does "AI-powered" actually mean in practice?
AI handles research, document drafting, data analysis and deadline tracking; senior consultants review, decide and take responsibility. You get faster turnaround and lower fees — with human accountability on every deliverable.
Do you work with businesses outside Delhi and Bengaluru?
Yes — filings, documentation, payroll and advisory are delivered remotely pan-India. Our Delhi and Bengaluru offices handle anything requiring physical presence.
How quickly can you take over our compliance?
Typically within two weeks: week one for document collection and a compliance health check, week two to set up your calendar and systems. Urgent deadlines can be handled immediately.
